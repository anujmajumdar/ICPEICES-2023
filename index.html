Conference Information

IEEE Power Electronics, Drives and Energy Systems (PEDES) is a biennial conference sponsored by four societies of IEEE - Industry Applications Society (IAS), Power Electronics Society (PELS), Industrial Electronics Society (IES), and Power & Energy Society (PES). The first edition of the conference was held in 1996 at New Delhi, India. It has subsequently been held in Perth Australia (1998), New Delhi (2006 & 2010), Bengaluru (2012), Mumbai (2014), Thiruvananthapuram (2016), Chennai (2018), Jaipur (2020), and the tenth in the series i.e. IEEE PEDES 2022 will be held at Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT) Jaipur, Rajasthan from 14-17 December 2022. The goal of PEDES is to provide a platform for conference, exhibition and tutorials on selected topics of contemporary interest.

The conference provides a unique forum for the exchange of knowledge and information among the professionals across the globe in the areas of power electronics, drives and energy systems – subjects which have greater significance owing to the emphasis on energy affected by the climate change. The conference aims at bringing together the users and researchers of electrical energy conversion systems and sub-systems with the emphasis on the technical content of the papers.

The conference sessions will highlight the latest research and development efforts enabling the minimization of technical and cost barriers. The scope of the conference includes all aspects of the design, manufacture, application of devices, circuits, and systems related to electrical energy.

Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements and will be eligible for further review for publication in IAS Transactions and magazine.

We look forward to meeting you in MNIT Jaipur at IEEE PEDES Conference 2022.

General Chairs Message – ICPEICES 2023

It is our true pleasure to invite you to join us at the 10th IEEE PEDES Conference being organized from 14-17 December, 2022. A biennial conference, PEDES 2022 is being organized in the beautiful city of Jaipur, India at MNIT Jaipur. PEDES is a principal conference for power electronics and is being sponsored by the IEEE - IAS, PELS, IES, and PES.

Known for its varied and innovative contents, PEDES 2022 is a leading technical conference in R10 for presenting latest research in power electronics & drives, energy systems, and control & automation. As has been before, PEDES has been a central platform where inquisitive and creative researchers from academia and industry gather to share their recent advancements and shape future trends of power electronic research. PEDES includes peer-reviewed research, which is presented through the technical presentations, poster presentations, and plenary discussions. With highly reputed and popular keynote and tutorial speakers lined up, this year’s conference is in line to follow the high standards that elevate PEDES from other conferences.

Rajesh Kumar, MNIT Jaipur, India
Akshay K. Rathore, Singapore Institute of Technology, Singapore
PEDES 2022, General Chairs
